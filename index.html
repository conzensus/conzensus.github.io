Tired of the indecision and endless group chats when planning your next outing? Let Conzensus bring some zen to your decision making!

Simply host a room with our app, and after your friends have joined, everyone swipes though which restaurants they like. Once everyone has voted, it shows you the restaurant everyone liked the most and how to get there. Whether it's a night out with friends or your significant other, Conzensus makes planning a breeze!
