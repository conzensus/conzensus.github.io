Conzensus is a fun and easy-to-use app designed to help groups of people decide on activities to do or where to eat. With Conzensus, users can select from a variety of broad categories that interest them, such as restaurants, bars, parks, or museums. The app then recommends individual places within these categories that users can swipe right if they like or swipe left if they dislike.

Whether you're planning a night out with friends or a family outing, Conzensus makes it easy to find the perfect activity or restaurant that everyone will enjoy. So why not download Conzensus today and start planning your next adventure?
